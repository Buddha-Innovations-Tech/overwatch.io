Our Story

A group of cybersecurity enthusiasts at heart, Overwatch Security founders noticed a shift in the market for cybersecurity solutions tailored to the needs of SMEs. SMEs which represent a large portion of the global businesses, face difficulty addressing their Security needs adequately. Choosing the right security product FIT that matches the business objectives and goals is a cumbersome task because of the cost, effort and strategic expertise associated with it.

From this shift, a company was born: Overwatch Security, Inc. Founded in the values of Secure Cyberspace, Secure Future. The notion that enterprises dont want to deal with choosing from an abundance of security solutions available in the market, the lack of skilled workforce and strategies necessary to properly execute their security metrics, and rather redirect their focus on what is important - The Business objectives and Goals.

A Single Platform Service can empower SMEs to become agile, efficient, and robust on the way they approach security while achieving their critical business objective and goal without interruption.

MDR Managed Detect and Respond

The purpose of a Managed detect and respond (MDR) is to identify, investigate, prioritize, and resolve issues that could affect the security of an organizations critical infrastructure and data. A well-developed and well-run MDR can perform real-time threat detection and incident response, with analysts that can deliver rapid security intelligence to stakeholders and senior management, identifying when an attack starts, who is attacking, how the attack is being conducted, and what data or systems are being compromised.

Our Product

01

SIEM Security information and event management

Our complete, evolved SIEM and threat defense platform delivers unsurpassed visibility, analytics and automated response capabilities from a single dashboard. These combined capabilities help security teams work more efficiently and effectively, up-leveling their threat hunting skills and enabling them to observe, orient, decide and act to threats faster, across their entire environment whether in the cloud, on premises. or virtual.

02

PAM Process Automation Manager

Our PAM Platform is an integrated solution that provides visibility and control over all privileged accounts and users. By uniting the broadest set of privileged security capabilities, the platform simplifies deployments, reduces costs, improves usability, and reduces privilege risks.

03

IAM Identity accessmanagement Solution

Digital transformation creates unprecedented authentication, access management and identity governance challenges for Enterprises. After all, more users need access to more systems from more devices than ever. Our IAM Platform helps organizations address these key challengesand the identity, access and compliance risks they createby combining multi-factor authentication, identity governance and user lifecycle management from a single, holistic platform.

04

FRI Fraud and Risk Intelligence

Fraud & Risk Intelligence Suite identifies and prevents fraud in real-time by blending risk-based decisions, predictive analytics, deep entity profiling, modern authentication and global fraud intelligence with the ability to correlate data across anti-fraud & dark web monitoring tools.

05

GCS Governance and Compliance Solution

Our Governance & Compliance Solution, empowers organizations to manage multiple dimensions of risk on one platform with on-premises and SaaS offerings, and quickly implement industry-standard processes and best practices for advanced risk management maturity, informed decision-making and enhanced business performance.
